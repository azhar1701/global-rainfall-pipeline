## Design Context

### Users
Hydrological researchers focusing on both real-time situational awareness and long-term trend analysis of global rainfall patterns.

### Brand Personality
Technical, precise, confident, yet simplified for rapid decision-making. The tone is clinical and authoritative ("Clean & Clear").

### Aesthetic Direction
Modern "Premium OLED Dark Theme" using glassmorphism. Focus on clarity, visual hierarchy, and high-performance data visualization. No specific brand references beyond the target of clinical precision.

### Design Principles
1. **Scientific Precision**: Data must be presented with high fidelity and confidence indicators.
2. **Operational Efficiency**: Layout should facilitate rapid decision-making through clear status indicators and workflow visualization.
3. **Inclusive Visibility**: High contrast and accessibility (WCAG AA) to ensure data is legible for all professional users.
4. **Data-Centric Simplicity**: Remove visual noise to keep the focus on maps, charts, and key metrics.
