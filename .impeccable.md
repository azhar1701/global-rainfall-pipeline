## Design Context

### Users
Environmental scientists, researchers, and data analysts who need to monitor global rainfall trends. They use this tool to fetch high-resolution satellite data (GPM, CHIRPS) via Google Earth Engine and perform statistical analysis (Mann-Kendall) on specific geographic areas.

### Brand Personality
1. **Precise**: Reflects the scientific accuracy of satellite data.
2. **Robust**: Handles large-scale data ingestion and processing with reliability.
3. **Analytical**: Prioritizes clear, data-driven visualizations for complex trends.

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Theme**: Premium Dark OLED dashboard (`#0B1120`).
- **Style**: Modern Glassmorphism using backdrop filters for layered depth.
- **Typography**: `Fira Sans` for UI readability and `Fira Code` for scientific and numerical precision.
- **References**: High-end satellite monitoring systems and professional engineering dashboards.
- **Color Palette**: Deep blue backgrounds with high-contrast primary blue (`#3B82F6`) and orange CTA (`#F97316`) accents.

### Design Principles
1. **Data over Decoration**: Every visual element must serve a purpose in clarifying spatial or temporal data.
2. **Progressive Disclosure**: Show high-level stats immediately; provide deep-dive raw data and trend analysis upon pipeline completion.
3. **Scientific Credibility**: Use precise typography and consistent units (mm) to maintain trust in the analytical output.
4. **Weighted Layout**: Balance complex controls (Sidebar) with large, visual analytical outputs (Map/Chart Split) to avoid overwhelming the user.
